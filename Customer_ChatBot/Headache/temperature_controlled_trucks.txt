Yes, every single truck we use for perishable transport is fully temperature controlled! We use active cooling systems to maintain the defined temperature ranges required to preserve the quality of your seafood. Our operational planning ensures that the cooling capacity of each vehicle perfectly matches the needs of the shipment, leaving nothing to chance.

We don't just assume the systems are working; we use monitoring to check compliance and log any deviations for our human oversight team to review. Regular maintenance is mandatory to ensure the reliability of these cooling units, supporting our commitment to food safety and transit integrity. By prioritizing this level of regulation, we reinforce the safety of your cargo and ensure a consistent cold chain!
