A refrigerated truck, often called a "reefer," is a specialized vehicle designed specifically to maintain a stable, controlled temperature during transit! These trucks are absolutely essential for transporting perishable goods like seafood because they have active cooling systems that run continuously. This prevents spoilage and ensures that your items stay within their safe temperature range from the warehouse to your door.

We assign these vehicles with great care, and our monitoring systems ensure that the cooling performance is meeting our strict standards. Our human oversight team verifies these conditions regularly to support food safety and quality preservation. Because these trucks are core assets to our cold chain, we follow mandatory maintenance and compliance checks to ensure they remain reliable for every shipment!
