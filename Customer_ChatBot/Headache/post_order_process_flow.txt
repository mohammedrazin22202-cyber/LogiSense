It’s exciting to get a new shipment started, and I’d love to walk you through what happens next! Once your request is placed, it enters a structured internal review where we confirm the quantity, destination, and feasibility. A member of our team will then reach out to you personally to finalize the confirmation and arrange for the 35% advance payment.

Once payment is received, the real action begins: we assign the vehicle, map the route, and perform final compliance checks before dispatching. After the order is on its way, you’ll receive your tracking info within 36 hours, and the final balance is simply collected at the time of delivery. This human-led workflow is designed to avoid errors and keep you informed every step of the way!
