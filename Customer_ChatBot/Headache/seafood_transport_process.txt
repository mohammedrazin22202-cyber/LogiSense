We take the transportation of seafood incredibly seriously, as we know that maintaining the cold chain is the only way to ensure quality and safety! Our process begins with a rigorous planning stage where temperature requirements are strictly defined based on the specific type of seafood you are shipping. We then assign specialized refrigerated vehicles that are perfectly equipped to handle the task, ensuring that handling is minimized to reduce any risk of exposure.

Throughout the journey, we continuously monitor transit conditions and optimize our routes based on duration and climate to preserve absolute freshness. Every shipment must comply with the highest food safety norms, and we perform regular operational checks to ensure no improper handling occurs. By standardizing our procedures and maintaining human oversight, we guarantee that freshness is protected from our facility all the way to your door!
