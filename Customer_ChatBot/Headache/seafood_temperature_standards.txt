The temperature we use is precisely tailored to the specific type of seafood you are transporting to ensure maximum safety! For wet seafood, we maintain a strictly controlled cooling environment, while frozen seafood requires sub-zero conditions to stay perfectly preserved. These ranges are defined during our meticulous planning phase and are enforced through the use of appropriate, high-quality refrigeration systems.

We monitor these conditions at every key stage of the journey, verifying that transit conditions align with our strict handling standards. Our system logs every detail, ensuring that no assumptions are made and that safety is never compromised. By prioritizing these precise temperature levels, we prevent spoilage and ensure that the quality of your shipment is maintained with absolute consistency!
