In the event that any internal or external rules are violated, we address the situation through a thorough internal review! We record every deviation transparently and use human oversight to investigate exactly what happened. This allows us to take immediate corrective actions and apply preventive measures to ensure it doesn't happen again.

We believe in total accountability, so we never conceal violations; instead, we assess the impact on quality and safety and document everything. This helps us learn and improve our processes to maintain the high level of trust our customers expect. By following these strict corrective procedures, we ensure that our operational integrity and safety remain our top priorities!
