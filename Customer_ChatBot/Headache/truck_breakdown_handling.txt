In the rare event of a truck breakdown, we have strict operational protocols in place to handle the situation immediately! Our systems record the incident, and our operational teams respond by initiating backup arrangements to ensure the safety of your shipment. Our top priority during a breakdown is assessing temperature preservation and moving quickly to minimize any quality risks.

We believe in factual communication, so we provide clear updates rather than speculative guesses while we work on the resolution. Our human oversight team manages the process to ensure that compliance is maintained and that corrective actions are fully logged. By following these procedures, we minimize the impact of transport failures and reinforce the reliability of our service even in challenging situations!
