The duration for which seafood can be safely transported depends entirely on the product type and the cooling method used! Fresh seafood typically requires much shorter, more aggressive transit timelines to stay perfect, whereas frozen goods can be transported over longer distances thanks to sub-zero temperature control. We define these duration limits during our planning phase and never assume unlimited transit time.

To manage these timelines, we select the most efficient routes and perform regular operational checks to verify compliance. Our handling procedures are optimized to minimize exposure, and we use continuous monitoring to detect any deviations that might affect the quality of the shipment. By balancing speed with precise temperature management, we ensure that your seafood remains fresh and reliable regardless of the distance!
