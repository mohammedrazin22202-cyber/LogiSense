Yes, we are fully equipped to handle both frozen and wet seafood shipments! We understand that these two categories have very different handling requirements and temperature needs, so we define separate ranges for each. During the planning phase, we assign the most appropriate refrigerated vehicles and ensure that transit is optimized for the specific sensitivity of the product.

To prevent any quality risks, we handle frozen and wet seafood with separate, standardized procedures and perform regular operational checks to verify conditions. Our human oversight team ensures that discipline is maintained throughout the journey, prioritizing safety and quality preservation for both types of cargo. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of reliability for all the seafood categories we transport!
